ESG Programs Like Hanwha Solar Forest are Helping to Combat Climate Change

10 Years of Hanwha Solar Forest :
Carbon-Neutral Forests to Combat Climate Change

Since 2011, Hanwha has planted over half a million trees using saplings grown in solar-powered nurseries without emitting carbon. Despite the pandemic, Hanwha continues its mission to sustainably combat climate change.

See how Hanwha and participants of the Hanwha Solar Forest have grown trees virtually using a contactless campaign mobile app which were then planted to create real, carbon-neutral forests.

Hanwha Solar Forest uses solar power to grow trees, contribute to afforestation and address climate change.

Find out more about Hanwha Solar forest at: https://www.hanwha.com/en/about_hanwha/hanwha_identity/energy_for_life_initiatives.html
